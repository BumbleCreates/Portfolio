The Redshift Project

I was asked by the band Redshift to help with their digital marketing and to build a website. I am currently finishing a website for the band which is coded in html,CSS and JavaScript. A preview of the website so far can be viewed here.

Step 1 - Establishing Target Audience and Goals

The first step I took was to have a semi-structured conversation with the band. Here I asked questions with the aim to establish the bands digital marketing pain points, their needs and try to understand their target audience. I took the notes from this session and created a mindmap using Miro. This mindmap was useful as a guide for further research such as competition anaylsis and SEO.
