Virtual Reality Gym Marketing Plan

Click Start Mini Project - In pairs we were tasked with designing a gym and producing a marketing strategy for that gym. It required identifying the target audience and tailoring the product to them, deciding how to market the product (how would this fit the target audience), deciding how to collect information for a clearer idea of the target audience for the product and how to reach them.
